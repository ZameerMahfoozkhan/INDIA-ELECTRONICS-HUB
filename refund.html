Refund Policy

Our refund and cancellation terms

Last Updated: May 2026

At India Electronics Hub, we strive to provide the best TV repair services. Please review our refund policy below.

1. Inspection Charges

Inspection and visiting charges are non-refundable once the technician has visited your location and performed the diagnosis. This charge covers the technician's time, travel, and diagnostic work regardless of whether you proceed with the repair.

2. Refund Eligibility

A refund may be applicable in the following situations:

  • Service Not Completed: If we are unable to complete the repair due to unavailability of parts or technical limitations, a refund of the repair charges (excluding inspection charges) will be provided
  • Duplicate Payment: If a duplicate payment is made accidentally, the excess amount will be refunded
  • Service Not Provided: If the technician fails to visit or the service was not provided after booking and payment, a full refund will be issued

3. Non-Refundable Situations

  • After successful completion of the repair service
  • If the customer declines repair after diagnosis (inspection charges still apply)
  • If the TV issue recurs due to external factors such as power surge, physical damage, or unauthorized tampering
  • If the customer provides incorrect information about the TV issue

4. Refund Process

  • To request a refund, contact us at 8005462960
  • Provide your service booking details and reason for refund request
  • Refund requests will be reviewed within 3-5 business days
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days

5. Cancellation

You may cancel a service booking before the technician's visit at no charge. Cancellations made after the technician has arrived will be subject to visiting/inspection charges.
