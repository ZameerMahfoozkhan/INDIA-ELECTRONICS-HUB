Common LCD TV Issues We Repair

  • Inverter Board Failure: The inverter powers the CCFL backlight in LCD TVs. When it fails, the screen goes dark while audio continues. We repair and replace faulty inverter boards.
  • CCFL Backlight Issues: Aging CCFL tubes can cause dim screens, pinkish tint, or uneven brightness. We replace faulty tubes and convert to LED backlighting where possible.
  • T-CON Board Problems: Horizontal or vertical lines, color banding, or half-screen display are often T-CON board issues that we expertly repair.
  • Panel Damage: Cracks, dead pixels, or widespread discoloration indicate panel damage. We offer panel replacement services for LCD TVs.
  • Power Supply Failure: TV not turning on, clicking sounds, or random shutdowns are usually power supply issues. Our technicians diagnose and fix SMPS problems efficiently.
  • Motherboard Issues: No response to remote, frozen screen, or boot loop problems can be motherboard-related. We perform component-level motherboard repairs.
  • Audio Problems: No sound, low volume, or distorted audio — we repair all LCD TV sound issues including amplifier and speaker problems.
