About India Electronics Hub

Your trusted partner for TV repair services in Lucknow

Who We Are

India Electronics Hub is a leading TV repair service provider based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. We specialize in repairing all types of televisions including LED TVs, LCD TVs, and Smart TVs from all major brands. Our team of certified and experienced technicians ensures that your TV is repaired quickly, efficiently, and at an affordable price.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fast, reliable, and affordable TV repair services to every household in Lucknow. We believe that quality repair services should be accessible to everyone, which is why we offer doorstep service with transparent pricing and no hidden charges.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Same Day Doorstep Service: We understand the importance of your TV in daily life. Our technicians reach your doorstep on the same day of booking.
  • Certified Technicians: Our team consists of highly trained and certified technicians with years of experience in TV repair.
  • Transparent Pricing: We provide upfront quotes after diagnosis. No hidden charges, no surprises.
  • Quality Parts: We use only genuine and high-quality replacement parts for all repairs.
  • All Brands Covered: Samsung, LG, Sony, Mi, OnePlus, TCL, Panasonic, Vu, and many more.
  • Warranty on Repairs: We stand behind our work with warranty coverage on repairs.
