Types of TV Screen Issues We Fix

  • Cracked Screen: Physical damage from impact or accidents. We source and replace screens for all TV sizes and brands in Lucknow.
  • Dead Pixels: Black or stuck pixels on the screen. Depending on the extent, we offer repair or replacement options.
  • Lines on Screen: Horizontal or vertical colored lines can often be fixed by repairing ribbon cables, T-CON board, or panel connections.
  • Screen Discoloration: Yellow spots, color patches, or uneven brightness can indicate backlight or panel issues that we diagnose and fix.
  • Half Screen Display: Only half the screen showing picture is typically a T-CON board issue that our technicians repair efficiently.
  • Dark Spots / Shadows: Dark areas on the screen can be caused by backlight failure, which we repair by replacing faulty LED strips.
  • Screen Flickering: Intermittent flickering can result from loose connections, faulty drivers, or panel issues — all of which we fix.

Brands & Sizes We Cover

Screen Repair vs Replacement

Not all screen issues require full panel replacement. Many problems like lines on screen, half-screen display, or flickering can be fixed through component-level repair at a much lower cost. Our honest technicians will always recommend the most cost-effective solution for your specific situation.
