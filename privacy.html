Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

Last Updated: May 2026

India Electronics Hub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our website or contact us for services:

  • Name: To identify you and address you properly
  • Phone Number: To contact you regarding your service request
  • Service Inquiry Details: Description of your TV issue for diagnosis purposes
  • Location: Your address in Lucknow for doorstep service

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your TV repair service requests
  • To contact you regarding your service booking, diagnosis, and updates
  • To provide doorstep service at your specified location
  • To improve our services based on customer feedback
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. Your data is shared only with our authorized technicians for the purpose of providing the requested repair service.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We ensure:

  • Secure handling and storage of all customer data
  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel only
  • Regular review of our data collection and storage practices

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, typically for the duration of the service period and a reasonable time thereafter for follow-up support.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
