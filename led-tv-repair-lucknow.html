Common LED TV Problems We Fix

  • Backlight Failure: If your TV has sound but no picture, or the screen is very dim, you likely have a backlight issue. We replace faulty LED strips and drivers efficiently.
  • Screen Flickering: Intermittent flickering or flashing can be caused by loose connections, faulty T-CON boards, or power supply issues. Our technicians identify and fix the root cause.
  • No Display / Blank Screen: A completely blank screen with power on can indicate panel, mainboard, or T-CON board issues. We diagnose the exact problem and provide the best solution.
  • Color Distortion: If colors appear washed out, have a tint, or show incorrect hues, this can be a panel or mainboard issue that we repair expertly.
  • Power Supply Issues: TV not turning on, randomly shutting off, or power cycling can be caused by capacitor failure or SMPS issues. We repair and replace faulty components.
  • Lines on Screen: Horizontal or vertical lines on the display can indicate panel or ribbon cable issues. We offer professional repair for such problems.
  • Sound Problems: No audio, distorted sound, or crackling noise — we fix all LED TV audio issues including speaker replacement.
