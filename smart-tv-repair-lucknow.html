Smart TV Problems We Fix

  • WiFi Connectivity Issues: TV not connecting to WiFi, frequent disconnections, or slow streaming. We diagnose and fix WiFi module and software issues.
  • App Crashes & Freezing: Apps not loading, crashing, or the TV freezing. We perform software updates, cache clearing, and firmware restoration.
  • Software & Firmware Updates: Outdated firmware causing bugs or compatibility issues. We update your Smart TV to the latest software version.
  • Screen Issues: No display, flickering, lines on screen, or dead pixels. We repair panel, T-CON, and backlight issues in Smart TVs.
  • Boot Loop / Stuck on Logo: TV stuck on the brand logo or continuously restarting. We fix firmware corruption and mainboard issues.
  • Remote Control Problems: Smart TV remote not working, Bluetooth pairing issues, or voice control malfunctions. We troubleshoot and resolve remote issues.
  • HDMI / USB Port Issues: Ports not detecting devices, no signal from set-top box or gaming console. We repair and replace faulty port components.
  • Audio Problems: No sound from built-in speakers, Bluetooth audio sync issues, or soundbar connectivity problems.
